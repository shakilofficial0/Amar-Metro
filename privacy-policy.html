Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 29, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Amar Metro! Your privacy is our top priority. This privacy policy outlines how we handle your information and ensure your data remains secure. Amar Metro operates offline and is committed to maintaining your privacy.

Data Collection

No Personal Data is Collected. Amar Metro does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app operates entirely offline, ensuring complete privacy.

Offline Functionality

Amar Metro does not require an internet connection to function. All features, including fare calculation, NFC support, and journey history, operate seamlessly offline. No online servers are accessed.

Ads and Tracking

We respect your time and privacy. Amar Metro is completely ad-free and does not use any form of tracking or analytics tools. There is no monitoring of user activity within the app.

Permissions

Amar Metro may request access to your device’s NFC functionality for interacting with metro systems. This permission is solely for enabling NFC-based features and is optional. No other permissions are required or used.

Security

Your security is important to us. By not collecting or storing any personal data, Amar Metro eliminates the risks associated with data breaches or misuse of personal information.

Third-Party Interactions

Amar Metro does not interact with any third-party services. Your data and activities remain entirely on your device, with no external dependencies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed to reflect new features or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted here, with the updated effective date at the top of the page.
